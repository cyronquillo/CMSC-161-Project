Instructions

1. Drag the object on the left side of the page until you match its shape
with the sillhouette located on the right side of the page.

2. Clicking the Show Overlay will load a superimposed transparent
version of the silhouette on the rotateable object. This action will add 10 seconds to the timer.

3. Click the check button once you think your answer is correct. The time
recorded on the timer will increase by 5 seconds if the answer is incorrect.

4. Once you have completed the 10 puzzles then your time would be stopped and recorded.

5. The top 5 shortest times recorded will be posted in the Best Times page.
